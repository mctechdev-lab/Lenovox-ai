About Lenovox AI

Why We Built Lenovox

Education in Nigeria and across Africa faces a massive access problem. Quality tutoring is expensive, study materials are hard to find, and students often study alone without support or motivation.

We built Lenovox AI to solve all of that in one app. With AI-powered tutoring, smart quizzes, a social community, rewards for learning, and tools that work on affordable Android phones — every student can now access world-class study support.

The LNX Coin Economy

LNX is the native coin of the Lenovox platform. You earn LNX by studying, completing quizzes, watching educational content, referring friends, and logging in daily. You spend LNX to access AI features.

This creates a real incentive to learn — students who study more earn more, and earn access to more powerful AI tools. The harder you work, the more the platform rewards you.

Our Mission
Democratise Quality Education
Every student in Nigeria and Africa deserves access to the same quality of tutoring as students in wealthy countries. AI makes this possible at scale.
Build a Learning Community
Studying alone is hard. The MeetUp social network connects students across Nigeria and Africa, creating study groups, friendly competition, and shared motivation.
Reward Hard Work
The LNX economy means students who put in the effort are rewarded with real value. Badges, leaderboards, and rankings celebrate academic achievement.
Represent Africa on the Global Stage
We are building world-class technology right here in Nigeria. Lenovox proves that Africa can lead the way in EdTech innovation.
