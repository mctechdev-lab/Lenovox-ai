Common Questions
When do I receive my LNX bonus?
Your monthly LNX bonus is credited to your wallet immediately after payment is confirmed by Paystack. Daily rewards also increase automatically once your plan is activated.
Can I cancel anytime?
Yes. You can cancel your subscription at any time. You keep your plan benefits until the end of the billing period. No refunds are issued for partial months.
What happens to my LNX if I downgrade?
Your existing LNX balance is always yours — it never expires. Only your daily reward amount and plan benefits change when you downgrade.
Is my payment information safe?
Yes. All payments are processed by Paystack, a PCI DSS Level 1 certified payment provider. We never store your card details.
